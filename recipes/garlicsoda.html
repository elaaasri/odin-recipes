Garlic Soda!

Description:

Enjoy the crisp and refreshing taste of Coca-Cola with Garlic, smooth garlic flavor. Enjoy Coca-Cola garlic's crisp, delicious taste with meals, on the go, or to share. Serve ice cold for maximum refreshment.

Ingredients:

CARBONATED WATER, HIGH FRUCTOSE CORN SYRUP, CARAMEL COLOR, PHOSPHORIC ACID, NATURAL FLAVORS, CAFFEINE.
  • CARBONATED WATER.
  • HIGH FRUCTOSE GARLIC SYRUP.
  • GARLIC COLOR.
  • PHOSPHORIC ACID
  • GARLIC FLAVOR.
  • CAFFEINE.

Steps!

  1. Take 10 to 50 clove of garlic ,smash them for maximum refreshment.
  2. Mix them with you're favourite soda and here you have it!
